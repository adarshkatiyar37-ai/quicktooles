About This Tool

The Base64 Decoder allows you to decode any Base64 encoded string into readable text instantly. This is extremely useful for developers, students, and creators who deal with encoded data or need to decode messages.

You can use this tool to debug encoded content, retrieve hidden text, or understand Base64 encoded messages. It works entirely in your browser, requires no software, and is free to use on all devices.

Simply paste your Base64 text, click "Decode", and get your readable content immediately. You can also clear the input to start fresh.
