🔤 Case Converter

Convert your text to Uppercase, Lowercase, Capitalize, or Sentence case


About This Tool

The Case Converter is a simple and fast tool to modify the format of your text. You can convert your text to uppercase, lowercase, capitalize each word, or apply sentence case.

This tool is useful for writers, students, developers, and anyone who needs to format text quickly without using complex software.

Just type or paste your text, select the desired conversion button, and get your formatted text instantly.
