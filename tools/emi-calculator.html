How to Use the EMI Calculator

The EMI Calculator helps you quickly compute your monthly loan payment based on principal amount, interest rate, and tenure.

Simply enter the loan amount, annual interest rate, and the tenure in months, then click "Calculate EMI" to get your monthly installment instantly.

This tool is useful for planning personal loans, home loans, or vehicle loans efficiently.
