How to Use the Image Cropper

This online tool allows you to crop any image quickly. Upload an image, select the area you want to keep by dragging your mouse on the image, and click "Crop & Download". The cropped image can be downloaded instantly.

This tool is useful for designers, content creators, and anyone who needs precise image crops for web or print.
