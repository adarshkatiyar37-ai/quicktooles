What is Lorem Ipsum?

Lorem Ipsum is simply dummy text of the printing and typesetting industry. It has been the industry's standard dummy text ever since the 1500s, when an unknown printer took a galley of type and scrambled it to make a type specimen book.

Unlike using "Content here, content here," Lorem Ipsum has a relatively normal distribution of letters, making it look like readable English. It is used as placeholder text so that designers can focus on the visual layout of a website, magazine, or app without being distracted by the actual content.

Why Do Designers Use Placeholder Text?

The primary reason to use a dummy text generator is to prevent the "readability" of the text from distracting the client or the designer from the visual hierarchy. If you use real content, people tend to read the words rather than looking at the font choice, line spacing, or grid alignment.

Using our advanced generator allows you to simulate exactly how much space a paragraph will take up on a mobile screen versus a desktop monitor, ensuring your UI/UX design is responsive and balanced.

The 2000-Year-Old History of Lorem Ipsum

Contrary to popular belief, Lorem Ipsum is not simply random text. It has roots in a piece of classical Latin literature from 45 BC, making it over 2000 years old.
