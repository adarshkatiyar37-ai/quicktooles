Frequently Asked Questions

Is it safe to upload confidential documents here?

Absolutely. The engine operates on a zero-retention infrastructure. The processed file is entirely deleted from the processing container the exact moment your download completes.

Can it process very large scanned files (800+ pages)?

Yes. The background optimization pipeline utilizes C-level PyMuPDF library streams, allowing the processing of multi-hundred-page files within a few seconds without interface lags.

What counts as a blank page for the automated script?

The system structurally checks for active text characters and graphical elements. Totally empty pages or blank sheets from scanners will be intercepted and cleanly filtered out.
